Welcome to Jahangirnagar University Course Registration System!

Discover a seamless and user-friendly experience for course registration at Jahangirnagar University. Our system is designed to simplify the process of selecting and enrolling in courses that align with your academic goals.

Key Features

  • Browse through a diverse range of courses offered by the University.
  • Easily creates student account and assign courses.
  • Shows real-time availability of courses and receive instant confirmations upon successful registration.

User Benefits

  • Enjoy the convenience of online registration from anywhere, anytime.
  • Receive personalized recommendations based on your academic program and interests.
  • Stay updated with important deadlines, announcements, and any changes to your course schedule.

User Guides and Support

  • New to our course registration system? Check out our user guides and tutorials for step-by-step instructions.
  • Our support team is ready to assist you with any questions or issues you may encounter during the registration process.
